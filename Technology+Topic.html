In the field of cybersecurity or security system, improvements of network technologies and technological literacy leading to the issue of growth of cybercrimes and cybercriminals/hackers. Cybercriminal/hackers is refer to "individuals who disrupt digital technologies and thereby damage the functioning of an institution or a society." ( Baecker,2019 ). Nowadays, they carried out attack that can evade existing security measures because of personal reason or political factors. while cybercrime is refer to "Cybercrime has been simply defined as a crime involving a computer network"(Kunal, 2023) Because digitalization of information allows information to be more accessible and visible, leading to increase of chance of unauthorized access and cyberthreats to gain personal information, to cause financial loss of individual or organization.

Furthermore, invasion or other form of attacks to cause disrupt business operation and critical infrastructure intend to shut down essential social service and fail business transactions could cause social and economic instability. Currently, there is a significant focus on impacts and risk of cybercrimes and developing advance preventive measures to protect individuals or communities from cyberattacks.
